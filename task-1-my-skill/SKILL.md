# Daily Fitness & Diet Logger

**Description:** A skill that takes raw daily activity and food intake brain-dumps and formats them into a clean, structured tracking log.

**Instructions:**
1. When the user provides a raw input of their daily physical activity and food intake, automatically trigger this skill.
2. Format the output with the following strict headers:
   - **🏃‍♂️ Physical Activity:** (Cleanly list step count, distance, or workouts).
   - **🍗 Diet & Nutrition:** (Cleanly list foods eaten and portions).
   - **🔥 Daily Summary:** (A one-sentence motivational summary of the day's effort).
3. Keep the tone encouraging and concise. Do not add extra conversational commentary outside the requested format.
